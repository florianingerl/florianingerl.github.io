Exercise with gerund

Make one sentence out of two.

Example:Timo earns a lot of money in his job. He is proud of it.

Timo is proud of earning a lot of money in his job.

  • Marvin is German. He is proud of it

    Marvin is proud of being German.

  • Kevin hasn't got any money to pay his bills. He is sorry for it.

    Kevin is sorry for not having any money to pay his bills.

  • Hermes hast lost his wife. He is angry about it.

    Hermes is angry about having lost his wife.

  • Anton might loose his job. He is worried about it.

    Anton is worried about loosing his job.

  • Mrs Rich might get robbed. She is afraid of it.

    Mrs Rich is afraid of getting robbed.

  • Tina can't play cards. She is bad at it.

    Tina is bad at playing cards.

  • Gustav hasn't bought his mother a birthday present. He is not sorry for it.

    Gustav isn't sorry for not having bought his mother a birthday present.
