Appointments and Cancellation Policy

The process of making an appointment is as follows:
  1. You send me your exercises and your associated theory script.
  2. If I am familiar with exercises like these, I will let you know and you can transfer the money in advance (via Paypal with the "Send money to friends" option*). In the subject line, please include your name and your appointment availability, for example something like "Math tutoring Elif free on Wed 1st Nov from 3 p.m. to 9 p.m or Saturday 1st Dec between 8 a.m. and 12 p.m. or Sunday all day"
  3. After your payment, I will give you an appointment confirmation according to your preferences (if it suits me) or I will suggest 2-3 appointments for the next few days
  4. We are looking forward to our meeting and you may send any missing or additional exercises/lecture notes.

!!!Attention!!! !!!Cancellation conditions!!!

In case of a cancellation (before the start of the tutoring), technical problems (e.g. unstable internet for more than 10 minutes) or a delay of more than 10 minutes on your part, the fee is due and I am not obligated to wait any longer - regardless of the duration of the booked appointment.

In the event of short-term cancellations or an internet failure on my part, you will receive an alternative appointment or a refund.

*Without the "Send money to a friend" option, there is a 2 euro fee for Paypal.

Teaching method

From the exercises that you send me, we choose the best ones. For explanations I use sketches, words, general rules and theorems (from your script), colors, formulas, examples and in the end, the task will explain itself through its solution and its interpretation in the factual context and its validation.
